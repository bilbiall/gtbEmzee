Upcoming Song Release

Posted on January 1, 2023

Emzee has announced that his latest single, "Big Dreams," will be released on February 14th. The song is a collaboration with fellow drill artist Lil Pump and features a heavy bassline and catchy chorus. Fans are already buzzing about the release, and many are speculating that it could be Emzee's biggest hit yet.
